    • Drive formatting

    Formatting a drive partition involves loss of data stored on the partition. Therefore, before you format the existing partition, you need to have a back up of data you required as it deletes whole data of drive partition.


    Format your drive using disk management


    Here are steps to get your drive formatted in Windows:

    Extend a partition


    1. Right-click on a partition.
    2. Tap "Format" .
    3. Follow the formatting wizard to set the file system and volume label.
