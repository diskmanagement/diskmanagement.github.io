    • Disk Management

    Disk management is a built-in Windows utility that allows you to manage and configure your computer's storage devices, such as hard drives and solid-state drives.

    To Open Disk Managment

    1. First go to the desktop and right-click on "This PC" or "My Computer" icon.
    2. Then, select "Manage".
    3. Now go to "Storage" in the left sidebar and click 'disk management'.

    What can you do with disk management?


    • Drive partition
    • Change in drive letter
    • Drive formatting
    • Initialize another new disk
    • Drive partition Contraction
    • Drive partition Expansion
    • Drive partition deletion
